University of Cape Town Mathematics Competition


Awards

The competition offers a number of awards to participants and schools:

Prizes and Gold Awards will be awarded to the top ten individuals in each grade, and the top three pairs.

Merit Awards will be awarded to the top 150 individuals in each standard, and the top 100 pairs.

A School Prize is presented to the highest-ranking individual in each school, unless that school has already won a Gold award.

All entrants will receive Certificates of Participation.

Trophies

Three trophies will be awarded:

The Mona Leeuwenberg Trophy, which commemorates the founder of the Competition, is awarded to the school with the best overall performance in the Competition.

The UCT Trophy will be awarded for the best performance by a school which has participated in the Competition at most twice before.

The award of these trophies will be determined by totalling the best 30 scores achieved by a school's entrants.

The Diane Tucker Trophy will be awarded to the best perfomance by a girl.

The Gold Award winners, and a selection of the winners of School Prizes, attend the Competition Prize Giving at the University of Cape Town.

Awards are made at the discretion of the Competition Director.
