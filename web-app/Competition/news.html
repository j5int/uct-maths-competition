We are excited to this year introduce an online entry as well as preparation system

  • Schools can directly enter the details of their participation into an online database
  • Schools will be able to edit details until the cut-off date
  • Learners entered as Individuals will be able to practice and prepare online
  • To this extend schools are encouraged to enter early
